How technology has improved the efficiency of use of water and the safety of the Panama Canal over the years.

About the Project

TECH FLOW SYSTEM is a STEM Week initiative that explores how innovation and digital tools have transformed water management and safety at The Panama Canal Watershed. This web app includes interactive diagrams, a timeline, and a quiz to test your knowledge!

Smart Water Management ensures efficient flow control.

Cross-fills help reuse water and conserve resources.

Transit Optimization reduces delays and boosts efficiency.

Drones for Surveillance monitor canal activity in real-time.

Digital Twins simulate canal operations for better planning.

Continuous Monitoring improves decision-making and safety.
